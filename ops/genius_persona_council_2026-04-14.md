# Genius Persona Council - 2026-04-14

## Room
- Richard Feynman: reduce the verdict to the fewest true branch points
- Steve Jobs: remove cognitive clutter and make the next action obvious
- Edward Tufte: show evidence where the decision is made
- Nassim Taleb: expose uncertainty instead of pretending certainty
- Grace Hopper: make the operator prepare the right inputs before escalation
- W. Edwards Deming: improve the flow that prevents rework
- Charlie Munger: invert the common failure modes first
- Judge

## Forced question
How should the NYC LL152 product change if a council of first-principles product thinkers optimizes for faster, safer owner decisions instead of broader content coverage?

## Feynman
- The product still wins on three branch points:
  - is the building likely covered
  - which gas-status branch applies
  - what filing path follows from that branch
- Anything that does not tighten one of those branches is noise.

## Jobs
- The first screen should not ask the user to browse a library of routes.
- It should let the user pick the blocker first:
  - due now
  - what to file next
  - no gas versus no active service
  - penalty or waiver
  - after inspection

## Tufte
- Evidence should sit next to the verdict, not in a hidden methodology layer.
- Every operational answer should expose official source anchors.

## Taleb
- The checker must show confidence, not just a verdict.
- Missing district, property classification, or gas-status detail should visibly lower confidence.

## Hopper
- A verdict without a preparation list creates support churn.
- Each scenario should end with a short `what to prepare next` checklist.

## Deming
- The site should reduce loopbacks.
- Owners should reach the lead form only after the product has narrowed the branch and listed the documents to gather.

## Munger
- Invert the avoidable mistakes:
  - mixing `no gas piping` with `no active gas service`
  - opening DOB NOW before the branch is explicit
  - trusting a broad property guess when the DOF class or district is still missing
  - treating partner help as proof

## Judge
- Final decision: `ship the product as a blocker-first decision surface with confidence, preparation checklists, and source anchors`
- Confidence: `9.1 / 10`

## Product decisions adopted
1. Home adds a `Pick the blocker` layer above the route library.
2. Checker results add:
   - confidence label
   - scenario-specific preparation checklist
   - official source anchors
3. Route pages add:
   - what to prepare next
   - official source anchors

## What not to ship from this council
- a generic thought-leadership section
- broad borough content expansion
- a methodology-heavy hero
- a lead form before the filing branch is narrowed
