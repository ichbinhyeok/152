# 04 Commercial Model

## Commercial thesis
This project should monetize from **LMP and compliance-help routing**, not from ads and not from a giant directory.

## Primary revenue paths

### LMP inspection or compliance leads
Best when:

- the building is covered
- the filing next step is inspection or certification related

### No-gas or no-active-gas-service help
Best when:

- the user is stuck on the right documentation path

## Revenue paths to avoid first
- generic NYC plumber directory
- phone-heavy compliance concierge
- broad borough SEO

## CTA matrix

### Checker verdict
Primary CTA:

- `Get LMP or filing help`

Secondary CTA:

- `See the exact filing next step`

### No-gas split
Primary CTA:

- `Get certification help`

### Penalty or waiver
Primary CTA:

- `Check waiver or filing options`

## First-cash model
1. a small number of routed compliance leads
2. sponsor outreach once checker and next-step pages show demand
3. later district or owner-portfolio packages if route traction appears
