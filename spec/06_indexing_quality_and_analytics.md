# 06 Indexing Quality And Analytics

## Indexing philosophy
Index only pages that are:

- checker or next-step specific
- current-rule aware
- source-backed
- commercially meaningful

## Pages that should index first
- home
- checker
- filing-next-step
- no-gas split
- extension-penalty-waiver
- after-inspection
- 2026-deadline

## Pages that should stay `noindex` at first
- district overlays
- unsafe-condition page
- broad LMP finder

## Event taxonomy
- `page_view`
- `checker_start`
- `checker_complete`
- `route_cta_click`
- `lead_form_open`
- `lead_submit_success`

## First proof-of-traction metrics
- checker completions
- clicks on `Get LMP or filing help`
- lead submissions from checker and next-step routes
