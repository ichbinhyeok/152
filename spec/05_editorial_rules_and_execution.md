# 05 Editorial Rules And Execution

## Editorial promise
The site explains the likely compliance next step under LL152. It does not pretend to provide legal certainty.

## Voice rules
- calm
- specific
- compliance-aware
- next-step-first
- never sensational

## Mandatory language rules
Use:

- `likely`
- `verify`
- `based on the current DOB guidance`
- `next filing step`

Avoid:

- `guaranteed compliance`
- `legal advice`
- `every building must`

## Claims the site can make
- a building is likely covered or not based on current official guidance
- different gas-status scenarios change the filing next step
- penalty, extension, and waiver logic differ by situation

## Claims the site must not make
- legal certainty
- professional sign-off
- universal penalty assumptions

## Execution order
1. Home and trust pages
2. Checker
3. Filing-next-step page
4. No-gas split page
5. Extension-penalty-waiver page
6. After-inspection page
7. Held routes later
